Geek Investments is a feature-rich, non-commercial social trading platform designed to simulate the experience of managing and sharing investment trades in a collaborative environment. Users can log in, record detailed trade information, share insights, and interact with others through likes and trade sharing, fostering a sense of community among investors. The application combines functionality with an intuitive user interface to deliver an engaging, educational experience.

User Authentication and Secure Access

Authentication ensures secure access, with users required to log in to utilize the platform. The system supports account creation, secure logins, and user-specific data access to maintain privacy and enhance security.

Trade Management

Users can add, view, edit, and delete trades with details such as ticker symbol, purchase price, number of shares, and additional notes. Each trade can include an uploaded image, adding visual context to the trade record.

Interactive Features

The platform includes social interaction capabilities, allowing users to "like" trades and share them selectively with other users. Likes are reflected in real-time, while sharing trades provides a collaborative dimension to the platform.

Trade Details and Insights

Users can view detailed trade information, including ticker, price, purchase date, and notes. The intuitive layout ensures important details are easy to access, creating a streamlined experience for users analyzing their investments.

Visual Feedback and Notifications

Toast notifications provide instant feedback on actions, such as adding, editing, deleting, or sharing a trade. These notifications improve usability by keeping users informed about the success or failure of their actions.

Responsive Design

Built using Bootstrap, the platform is fully responsive, providing a seamless experience across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices. The layout adapts dynamically to different screen sizes, ensuring accessibility and usability on any device.

Educational and Non-Commercial

This project serves as an educational tool to demonstrate the functionality and design of a social trading platform. It is not intended for commercial use but showcases key features and technical capabilities in a visually appealing and user-friendly manner.

Geek Investments offers a well-rounded, interactive simulation of a trading platform, ideal for users looking to understand the core functionalities of managing and sharing trades in a collaborative ecosystem.
